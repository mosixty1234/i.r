Welcome to I. R. Lesolang Secondary School

Welcome to the esteemed I. R. Lesolang Secondary School, an integral pillar of the educational landscape in our vibrant community. I. R. Lesolang Secondary School is a bustling hub of intellectual growth and development, operating under the aegis of the Tshwane West Education District, which serves as a beacon of educational excellence in the Gauteng region.

We are committed to fostering an environment conducive to learning, growth, and advancement for all our students.

About Us

Our Mission

To ensure quality learning and teaching takes place in the modern classroom, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Vision

Ensuring every learner does well at school and leaves our institution with the knowledge, skills and qualifications that will give them the best chance of success in adult life.

Our Curriculum

The South African secondary school curriculum, known as the Further Education and Training phase, includes compulsory subjects as well as a choice of optional subjects. The National Senior Certificate is awarded at the end of Grade 12.

Compulsory Subjects

  • Home Language (Setswana or IsiZulu)
  • First Additional Language
  • Life Orientation
  • Mathematics or Mathematical Literacy

Optional Subjects

  • History
  • Geography
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Accounting
  • Physical Sciences
  • Life Sciences
  • Consumer Studies
  • Tourism
